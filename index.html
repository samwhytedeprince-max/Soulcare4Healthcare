06 — Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is Soulcare Science of Health? +

Soulcare Science of Health is a universal consciousness framework that illuminates the mind through three integrated dimensions: Neuro-Psychic, Somato-Physiological, and Eco-Social Health — bridging scripture-based wisdom with the science of human consciousness.

How long does the programme run? +

Our programmes are designed as 365-day continuous journeys. We believe deep, lasting transformation requires sustained engagement rather than short courses. Monthly plans give you ongoing access to all resources and sessions.

Is this programme religious or scientific? +

Soulcare uniquely bridges both worlds. We use scriptural truth as our foundation while applying contemporary understanding of consciousness, psychology, and somatic science. The result is a holistic system that is neither purely religious nor purely secular.

Can I start with the free Discovery tier? +

Absolutely. The Discovery tier is your gateway — one free consultation session, introductory materials, and group webinar access with no commitment required. Many of our most transformed members started exactly here.

Where are sessions held? +

Sessions are available both in-person at our Anambra base and online via webinar for participants across Nigeria and beyond. Contact us to confirm current scheduling and available formats.
